About Us

An Enabling Space for Networking & Knowledge Dissemination

A smart purpose-built conference and events facility on a 6000 sq. ft. built-up area with 450 seating capacity. Equipped with advanced audio-visual technology, meticulously designed acoustics, distinctive lighting features, and an impressive LED wall, this spacious and versatile canvas provides seamless connectivity and shall be an ideal venue for an academic symposium, cutting-edge tech summit, or theatrical show. A fully air-conditioned space where architecture, technology, and aesthetics blend to create a dynamic learning space, Sarabhai Kalam Theater is poised to set the stage for engaging discussions and memorable networking opportunities
